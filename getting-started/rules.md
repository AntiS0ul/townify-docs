# Server Rules

!!! warning "Staff: customize this page"
    These are sensible default rules for a Towny survival-economy server. **Review and edit them to match how you actually run TownifyMC** before you point players here, and make sure they line up with your in-game `/rules` and your punishment ladder.

Breaking these rules can result in a warning, mute, kick, temp-ban, or permanent ban depending on severity and history. Staff decisions are final, but you can always appeal in [Discord](https://discord.gg/townifymc).

## The big ones

1. **No cheating.** No hacked clients, X-ray, fly hacks, killaura, auto-clickers, macros, or any unfair modification. Approved QoL mods (minimaps without entity radar, etc.) are fine — ask if unsure.
2. **No griefing or stealing.** Don't destroy, alter, or steal from builds and chests that aren't yours. Towny protects claimed land; don't try to work around it.
3. **Be respectful.** No harassment, hate speech, slurs, discrimination, or targeted bullying. Treat everyone with basic decency.
4. **Keep chat civil.** No excessive spam, caps, advertising other servers, or NSFW content.
5. **No scamming.** Trades and deals are at your own risk, but deliberately defrauding players (fake shops, rigged deals, chargebacks) is bannable.
6. **No exploiting bugs.** Found a duplication glitch or money exploit? **Report it** — don't abuse it. Abusing exploits is treated as cheating.

## Towny & land

- Respect other towns' borders and embassies.
- War and sieges happen **only** through the official [Siege War](../towns/war.md) system. No "unofficial" raiding by tunneling under claims or trapping players.
- Don't build offensive or inappropriate structures (especially near spawn or other towns).
- **Lag machines** (redstone clocks, mob stacking past limits, excessive entities) are not allowed.

## Accounts & fair play

- **One account per person** for ranking and rewards, unless staff approve alts. Using alts to farm vote rewards or rankup is not allowed.
- Don't share accounts.
- **AFK farming**: light AFK is fine; rigging up automated AFK money/job farms to bypass activity checks is not.

## Reporting

See someone breaking the rules?

- In-game: open a ticket or use `/report <player> <reason>` :material-wrench: if available.
- [Discord](https://discord.gg/townifymc): use the support/tickets channel.

Provide screenshots or clips where you can — it helps staff act quickly and fairly.
