# Fishing

Fishing on TownifyMC is its own progression system. Beyond catching food, you'll reel in **rare, custom fish** with their own rarities — and compete in **timed fishing competitions** for money and gem prizes.

## Custom fish & rarities

Cast a line and you won't just catch the usual cod and salmon. TownifyMC uses an expanded fishing system with **custom fish sorted into rarity tiers** — common fish are easy and frequent, while rare and legendary catches are prized (and valuable).

Each fish can have:

- A **rarity** (common → rare → and beyond)
- A **size/length** that varies catch to catch
- **Value** when sold — rarer, bigger fish are worth more

```
/emf shop       # sell your catches / fishing shop :material-wrench:
/emf help       # fishing commands :material-wrench:
```

!!! tip "Rare fish = real money"
    Don't just toss your catches. Rare fish sell for a premium, and some are sought-after by collectors and for competitions. Hang onto the good ones.

## Rods & bait

Better gear means better catches. The fishing system supports:

- **Custom rods** — specialized fishing rods with their own perks.
- **Bait** — apply bait to your rod to influence what you catch, improving your odds of landing rarer fish.

Experiment with rod-and-bait combos to target the catches you want — whether that's bulk common fish to sell or a shot at something legendary.

## Fishing competitions

This is where fishing gets competitive. TownifyMC runs **timed fishing competitions** that kick off periodically. When one starts, everyone fishing is in the running.

### How competitions work

- A competition **announces** in chat and runs for a set time.
- Depending on the competition **type**, you're competing to catch:
    - The **largest** fish, or
    - The **most** fish, or
    - The **rarest** / most valuable catch, or
    - A **specific** target fish.
- When the timer ends, the **top finishers win prizes** — typically **money and Gems**, scaled by placement.

```
/emf top        # see the current competition leaderboard :material-wrench:
```

!!! info "Watch for the announcement"
    Competitions are time-limited events. When you see the announcement in chat, grab your rod and get to the water — that window is your shot at the prize pool.

## Why fish?

| Reason | Payoff |
|--------|--------|
| **Fisher [job](../progression/jobs.md)** | Earn job money on every catch |
| **Fishing [skill](../progression/skills.md)** | Level your Fishing skill passively |
| **Selling catches** | Rare fish are valuable money |
| **Competitions** | Money & Gem prizes for top finishers |
| **Relaxing** | Honestly, it's a chill way to play and earn |

Stack it up: a single fishing session pays your **Fisher job**, levels your **Fishing skill**, stocks **sellable rare fish**, and enters you into any **active competition** — all while being one of the most laid-back activities on the server.
