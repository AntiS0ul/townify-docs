# Economy & Currencies

TownifyMC runs on **three currencies**, each earned and spent differently. Knowing what each one is for saves you a lot of confusion.

## The three currencies

| Currency | Symbol | What it's for | How you earn it |
|----------|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Money** | <span class="chip money">$</span> | The main economy — ranking up, shops, town costs, claims | Jobs, selling items, player trade |
| **Gems** | <span class="chip gems">Gems</span> | Premium currency for special purchases | Voting, events, crates, store :material-wrench: |
| **Vote Points** | <span class="chip points">Points</span> | Spent in the vote shop | Voting for the server |

### :material-cash: Money (`$`)

This is the currency that runs everything day-to-day. The entire [rank ladder](ranks.md) is priced in money, town and claim costs are in money, and most shops trade in money.

```
/bal            # check your balance
/baltop         # see the richest players
/pay <player> <amount>   # send money to someone
```

**Where money comes from:**

- [**Jobs**](jobs.md) — your primary, reliable income
- Selling items at [`/shop`](../money/shops.md) or to other players
- [Player markets and auctions](../money/shops.md)
- [Fishing competitions](../money/fishing.md)
- [Daily quests](quests.md) and [voting](../rewards/voting.md) payouts

### :material-diamond-stone: Gems

Gems are a **premium currency** — rarer than money and used for higher-value or special purchases. You'll typically earn them from voting rewards, events, and crates, and they may be available from the server store.

```
/gems           # check your gem balance :material-wrench:
```

!!! note
    Exact gem sources and the gem shop are configured server-side. Check the in-game menus and [Discord](https://discord.gg/townifymc) for the current ways to earn and spend Gems.

### :material-star-circle: Vote Points

Vote Points are earned by [**voting**](../rewards/voting.md) for the server on listing sites. Spend them in the **vote shop** on useful items and perks. They're the easiest "free" currency on the server — just vote daily.

```
/vote           # see vote links and your points
```

See the [Voting](../rewards/voting.md) page for the full breakdown of vote rewards.

## Smart money tips for new players

1. **Don't hoard early.** Spend your first earnings on ranking up — early ranks are cheap and unlock tools that make earning faster.
2. **Vote every day.** It's free money, points, and crate keys for ~1 minute of effort.
3. **Pick complementary jobs.** Once you unlock a [2nd and 3rd job slot](jobs.md), choose jobs that reward the same activities so a single play session pays multiple ways.
4. **Watch the market.** Prices at player shops fluctuate. Buy low, sell high, and undercut the server shop where you can.
5. **The ladder gets steep fast.** Ranks 1–10 are reachable through normal play; ranks 20+ run into the tens of millions. That's intentional endgame content — don't be discouraged, just settle in.
