## A taste of what's here

TownifyMC runs a deep plugin ecosystem. A few highlights you'll run into:

- **:material-bag-personal: Backpacks & Vaults** — portable and stored inventory space that grows with your rank.
- **:material-party-popper: Cosmetics** — particle trails, gadgets, and visual flair.
- **:material-heart: Marriage** — pair up with another player for perks.
- **:material-glass-mug-variant: Brewing** — craft custom brews with the BreweryX system.
- **:material-grave-stone: Death Chests** — your items are protected in a grave when you die, instead of scattering everywhere.
