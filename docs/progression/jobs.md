# Jobs

Jobs are your **primary source of income** on TownifyMC. You get paid for normal gameplay actions, and each job can level up as you use it.

## How jobs work

```
/jobs
/jobs join <job>
/jobs leave <job>
/jobs stats
/jobs browse
```

- You earn money and job XP each time you perform an action your job rewards.
- Job payouts scale as the job levels up.
- Job slots are rank-gated.

### Job slots by rank

| Slots | Unlocked at | Rank |
|------:|-------------|------|
| 1 | Start | <span class="tier-common">Pleb</span> |
| 2 | <span class="chip money">$60,000</span> | <span class="tier-uncommon">Merchant</span> (#7) |
| 3 | <span class="chip money">$67,000,000</span> | <span class="tier-legendary">Duke</span> (#23) |

## The 12 jobs

| Job | You get paid for... | Good if you like... |
|-----|---------------------|---------------------|
| **Miner** | Mining ores and stone | Caving, branch mining, gathering |
| **Farmer** | Harvesting crops | Farms and passive setups |
| **Lumberjack** | Chopping trees | Wood gathering and tree farms |
| **Rancher** | Breeding and raising animals | Animal farms and food production |
| **Excavator** | Digging dirt, sand, and gravel | Terraforming and big digs |
| **Builder** | Placing blocks and building | Construction and town projects |
| **Hunter** | Killing hostile mobs | Combat and mob grinding |
| **Fisher** | Catching fish | Relaxed play and fishing competitions |
| **Blacksmith** | Smelting and crafting | Processing raw materials |
| **Cook** | Cooking food | Food economy and brewing-adjacent play |
| **Explorer** | Discovering and traveling | Roaming and mapping the world |
| **Arcanist** | Enchanting and magical actions | Enchanting and utility play |

!!! tip "Picking your first job"
    Miner, Lumberjack, and Farmer are reliable starters because they pay for basic resource gathering.
