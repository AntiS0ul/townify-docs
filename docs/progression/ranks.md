# The Rank Ladder

TownifyMC has a **27-rank ladder**. You start as a **Pleb** and climb all the way to **Celestial**, unlocking commands, perks, storage, travel, and flight along the way.

## How ranking up works

Ranks are unlocked with **money** (`$`). To rank up:

```
/rankup
```

Open the rank menu to see your progress:

```
/ranks
```

!!! tip "Auto rank-up"
    The rank menu has an auto rank-up toggle. Enable it and you will automatically rank up when you meet the requirements.

## Major unlocks

- **Countryman (#5)** unlocks creating a town.
- **Warrior (#10)** unlocks creating a nation.
- **Merchant (#7)** unlocks your 2nd job slot.
- **Duke (#23)** unlocks your 3rd job slot.
- **Marquess (#22)** starts flight access with fly inside claims.
- **Celestial (#27)** unlocks global fly.

## Milestones at a glance

- **Ranks 1-5:** starter ranks, portable workstations, and town creation.
- **Ranks 6-10:** more workstations, a second job slot, and nation creation.
- **Ranks 11-15:** player vaults, enderchest access, backpacks, nicknames, and walk speed.
- **Ranks 16-20:** cosmetic/social perks, player warps, and more storage.
- **Ranks 21-27:** vertical travel, flight, top-tier storage, and full DrawOnMap access.
