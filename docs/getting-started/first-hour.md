# Your First Hour

A practical checklist for brand-new players. Follow this and you'll have a job, some money, and a plan within your first session.

## 1. Get oriented at spawn

When you join, you'll start at **spawn** as a **Pleb** (the starting rank). Take a moment to look around — spawn usually has signs, NPCs, and portals to key areas.

Handy commands to know right away:

- `/spawn` — teleport back to spawn anytime
- `/rules` — read the server rules (do this!)
- `/help` — built-in command help
- `/menu` or `/cmenu` — opens the main server menu (if available) :material-wrench:

## 2. Join a job

As a Pleb you have **1 job slot**. Jobs are your main early income.

```
/jobs
```

This opens the jobs menu. Browse the 12 available jobs, pick one that matches how you like to play, and join it. (See the full [Jobs](../progression/jobs.md) page for what each one pays you for.)

!!! tip "Which job first?"
    **Miner**, **Lumberjack**, and **Farmer** are great starters — they earn steadily from things you'll be doing anyway while gathering early resources. You'll unlock more job slots as you rank up.

## 3. Earn your first money

Go do your job! Mine, chop, farm, hunt — whatever your job rewards. Every qualifying action pays you a little **money** (`$`).

Check your balance any time:

```
/bal
```

You can also sell gathered items at the server shop (see [Shops & Markets](../money/shops.md)):

```
/shop
```

## 4. Rank up

Once you've saved **$500**, take your first step up the ladder:

```
/rankup
```

This promotes you from **Pleb** to **Commoner** and unlocks `/craft` (a portable crafting table). Open the rank menu any time to see what's next:

```
/ranks
```

!!! info "Auto rank-up"
    There's an **auto rank-up** toggle in the rank menu. Turn it on and you'll be promoted automatically the moment you can afford the next rank — no need to keep running `/rankup`.

## 5. Set your goals

Here's what the early ladder unlocks, so you know what you're working toward:

| Rank | Cost | Unlocks |
|------|------|---------|
| Commoner | <span class="chip money">$500</span> | `/craft` |
| Citizen | <span class="chip money">$1,500</span> | `/stonecutter` |
| Servant | <span class="chip money">$4,000</span> | `/loom`, Night Vision |
| **Countryman** | <span class="chip money">$10,000</span> | **Create a Town** 🏘️ |

Reaching **Countryman** is the first big milestone — it lets you found your own town. See the full [Rank Ladder](../progression/ranks.md) for all 27 ranks.

## 6. Find your people

Minecraft is more fun with others:

- Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/townifymc) for announcements, help, and to find a town to join.
- Use `/towny` and look at the [live map](https://map.townifymc.xyz) to find existing towns recruiting members. **Joining an established town** is often the fastest way to get set up — you skip the upfront cost of founding your own.
