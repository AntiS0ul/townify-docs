# Server Rules

These are the official TownifyMC rules, mirrored from the [staff rules document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/14HHn9wilr0cdiQ8LJSB2iW0QDYKiCW7G31hUzJfj26M/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.vhrwfq32awta). Staff rulings and future updates should follow the canonical document if it changes.

## Chat Rules

1. Do not harass or abuse other players. This includes but is not limited to malicious signage, written books, messages, DMs, chats, and auction house listings.
2. Do not spam, advertise, use excessive capslock, use alternate fonts, or otherwise negatively impact the readability of chat.
3. English is required in all publicly visible chats, whether in-game or on Discord.
4. Discrimination, hate speech, derogatory language, and slurs of any kind are forbidden. This includes but is not limited to Town Chat, messages, DMs, Marry Chat, MapArt, signage, written books, and auction house listings.
5. Depictions of lewd or pornographic content, gore, drugs or drug use, abuse of a person or animal, extremist political iconography, or exploitative depictions of minors are forbidden.
6. Do not use excessive or targeted profanity.
7. Do not discuss politics, polarizing topics, or personal traumas in publicly visible chats. This includes but is not limited to Town Chat, messages, DMs, Marry Chat, MapArt, signage, written books, and auction house listings.
8. Do not dox or otherwise disclose private personal information belonging to yourself or anyone else.
9. Do not impersonate staff or other players in any way.
10. Complaints, suggestions, and other feedback belong in the Discord ticket system. Destructive or non-constructive criticism is forbidden in publicly visible chats.
11. Respect boundaries set by others in chat. If another person asks you to stop discussing something in-game or in Discord, failing to respect that boundary may be punished as malicious action.
12. These rules are subject to change at the discretion of the staff team in order to better protect the community.

## In-Game Rules

1. Hacking, X-Ray, unapproved scripts or mods, and other tools that may result in an unfair advantage are forbidden. This includes but is not limited to hacked or cracked clients, use of alt accounts, and use of a VPN to bypass alt detection.
2. Machines or contraptions that can be used automatically, without player interaction, to kill, destroy, place, or otherwise perform actions are forbidden. Auto-smelters, auto-brewing, and egg farms are exempt. Bypassing AFK checks to continuously run these machines is strictly forbidden.
3. All written agreements are binding. Contracts made in chats, DMs, messages, written books, signage, Discord, or other messages must be honored.
4. Do not scam in trades, auctions, player shops, or any other transaction. This includes misleading item names, price gouging, misrepresentation of listed or sold items, false advertising, and other intentionally misleading transactions.
5. Do not TP kill, trap kill, kill steal, or otherwise mislead players into PvP zones.
6. Do not exploit bugs or glitches, or distribute bugs and glitches to other players. If a feature is working disproportionately above its intended value and a player abuses it instead of reporting it, that abuse is punishable as exploitation.
7. In-game to real-world money trading, known as RMT, is forbidden.
8. Do not distribute, showcase, or display items with inflammatory, inappropriate, or rule-breaking names.
9. Do not set inflammatory, inappropriate, or rule-breaking nicknames that would encourage others to break chat rules.

## Macros and Griefing Rules

1. Autoclickers, macros, and other automated tools are not permitted to be used in an AFK-able fashion. They must be limited to 8 CPS, and you must be able to respond to staff within 2 minutes while using them.
2. Grief reports must be made by the owner of the town or unclaimed build that was griefed. If the owner has been offline for 7 days or longer, any player may report it.
3. Do not break or place blocks in towns or unclaimed builds without permission from the owner of the unclaimed build or staff of the town in question.
4. Mayors must give 72 hours' notice before removing a player from a town or clearing their build within a claimed plot.
5. Do not grief, deface, or collect resources from the Towny Overworld. Use resource worlds instead.
6. Raiding is forbidden in any claimed land. You may not grief to gain access to unlocked chests within an unclaimed space.
7. Do not grief, deface, or gather resources from a claimed Province. Staff will roll back grief within a claimed Province upon request.
8. Do not build or destroy within 5 chunks of a claimed town border.
9. Towns take precedence in the Province they have claimed. Player builds within that Province may be moved or rolled back by staff upon request, within reason.
10. Intentionally claiming over a build within a Province to maliciously prevent a player from creating their town is considered grief and is forbidden. In disputed ownership cases or unclear Province claim intent, staff may make an official ruling.

## Staff Rulings

1. In unclear, incomplete, or disputed rule cases, rulings defer to staff discretion.
2. If a player has a negative impact on the server or community, staff reserves the right to remove them at their discretion.
3. If a player feels they have been unfairly punished, they may appeal via ticket in Discord or personally contact a higher staff member by DM.
4. If real-life circumstances such as an emergency, natural disaster, or death of a loved one prevent you from upkeeping your town or nation, you may contact staff and request that upkeep be paused for the duration of those circumstances.
